About AfriDev Explorer

Making African developer talent more visible to the world.

Why AfriDev Explorer?

Africa has over 700,000 software developers on GitHub — and that number grows every year. Yet African tech talent remains largely invisible to the global developer community. AfriDev Explorer exists to change that: to surface the incredible work happening across 54 African nations, connect developers with opportunity, and celebrate the people building Africa's digital future.
